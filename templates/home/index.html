About Us

Techspace is the “of the students, by the students, for the students” technical society of USICT that works towards promoting discussion, creative collaboration, research and skill development.
We aim to provide an environment where students can tinker with ideas, experiment and capitalize on the power of a strong community.

Our vision is:
  • To inculcate the culture of sharing knowledge and values
  • To be the best place to nurture oneself

Cogitans

Cogitans is the web development club of U.S.I.C.T. under Techspace;
It is a community of senior and junior full stack developers building responsive and reactive front-end along with complex back-end to make solutions for a user base which is largest among the technical society. 60% of the user base prefers website over apps due to the minimal time it takes to convey the message or get the work done.
Members work together on live projects and solve real-world problems and participate in various hackathons on the national level.

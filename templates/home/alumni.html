ALUMS REPO

BTech., MTech., BCA, MCA, PhD. and all courses at USICT

With a Sense of Belonging to our Alma Mater, We are initiating the web portal of Alumni association of USICT. This is a repository of all members of the Alumni Association. The repository would contain a database of all the members, consisting of their details, such as where they work, their job fields, their areas of specializations and information regarding their time at our college. We welcome all the ex-students of USICT to join the community and help us move forward our own alma mater.

ASK ALUMS

Are you looking for some guidance from Alums of our own college, who have been in your shoes before. This is a weekly digest, wherein students would be able to ask the alumni queries they wish to solve. The queries may range from doubts about college lives, projects, submissions, reports and anything under the sun. Alumni are free to answer whenever they are able to. The students may ask a question to a particular alumnus, or put in their doubt as a general doubt, open to be answered by any alumni. The alumni can put up questions for the students as well. Submit your query here. An alum will reply back to your query with in a week.

ALUMS RESEARCH MONETARY SCHOLARSHIP (ARMS)

This initiative aims at building research and technical skills in the students. This scholarship will be bestowed to the students upon carrying out a project meeting certain precise conditions, which would be laid down soon. Applications for this fellowship would open in the month of December, the cream off process will take place in April and the fellowship will commence in the month of June. The project deadline would be till September and final submission would transpire at InfoXpression, wherein the project would be analysed. A student who concludes this fellowship successfully would be known as ARMS Fellow.

For early signup, submit the ARMS Fellowship Interest Form.


Not Just Another Fellow (NinJA Fellow)

This program is an initiative for the students having skills not restricted to the tech world. Here, the students would be steered to build projects by being causative to various verticals of the industry.

For early signup, submit the NinJa Fellowship Interest Form.
